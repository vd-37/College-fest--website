Welcome to Varchasva

A fest like never before

Varchasva is a Techno-Cultural festival of SJBIT being conducted for 2 days with a wide spread of Cultural and Technical events, co-ordinating over 2500 students and faculties from various colleges.This festival paves way for the students to showcase their talents and exhibit their potential with emerging and innovative ideas which throws light on collaborative spirit. A wide range of activities includes competition in sporting events, performing arts , technical contests ,etc enabling students across all the departments to actively take part and glorify the purpose of the event.
